What is Bookville?

      Bookville is website that I created for book recommendations for different types of books and readers. I will have different book lists for different types of book (ex. adventure fantasy, history, non-fiction, exct.) If you don't know what to read, or just are really bored with your book, you can look at the lists I have on here and maybe pick a new one. You can also look at my tips and tricks for making a book interesting.

Getting an Account

      You would think that you don't need an account for this website, and you would be right. However, getting an account will help us suggest different books for you . It also allows you to save discussions so you can talk on them later.

Discussions

      Discussions are like group chats that you can set up and join in. They are just chats, where a bunch of people talking about a specific book or genre, where you can add your opinion.You can also make your own, and only invite special people, or make it public, where anyone can join.
