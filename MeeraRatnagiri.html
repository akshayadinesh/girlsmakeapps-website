Soccer:

Soccer is a huge part of my life and I love to play. I have been playing scoccer my whole life and I have been playing for a travel team for the last four years. I have also watched lots of soccer on tv and in stadiums. My favorite womens team is the US women's soccer team. They are a very succesful team that has won the World cup three times and they have won four Olympic gold medals. The captain of the team is Carli Lloyd but my favorite player is Alex Morgan(#13).My favorite mens soccer team is Manchester United and my favorite player in Christiano Ronaldo.

Music:

I play two instruments, trumpet and cello. I play the trumpet in the school band and I play the cello in an orchestra called DYSO(Delaware Youth Symphony Orchestra) I have played trumpet for four years and I have played the cello for six years.

Animals:

I love lots of animals but my top five are koalas, horses, meerkats, pandas, elephants.

Food:

Food is the most amazing thing that we all need to survive, my favorite foods are sweets and fettuccine alfredo. The fettuccine alfredo from Olive Graden is so good!

Travel
